The Ethiopian Institute of Architecture Building Construction and City Development,EiABC, is located in Addis Ababa Lideta sub-city, adjacent to the main east-west artery of the city of Addis Ababa. In recent years the institute acquired new buildings that include the Urban Building situated in the east section of the institute's compound, Container Building situated adjacent to the institute's sport field and the New Building(White House) located on the north section of the institute's compound. Among these buildings specifically the EBuilding (white house or the new building) comprises class rooms, lecture halls, studios, staff rooms, laboratories and Staff cafeteria.
